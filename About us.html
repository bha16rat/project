  Our History

  Community Bank of Kolkata known for it's trust, security and progress was opened on 25th December 1984 on the auspicious day "CHIRSTMAS DAY". Our   first branch was opened in kolkata name Bank of Kolkata. Soon, after first 6 years of success we opened few more brances in india and after 10 years we   renamed it as Community Bank of Kolkata known CBK Bank.


  Our growth

  Starting from kolkata we have spreaded our brances in india. At the time between 1984 to 2000 there were only 12 brances in india. But now we have   successfully opened more than 1500 branches in india and that is the most number of brances opened by any bank. We are planning to expand our   branches to all over the world, so that our customer can enjoy the our facilities there also

          


  Our Associate Partners

  As we were moving up several companies started partnership with us. Talking about Automobile companies, Online Shopping to mobile companies and   many more.
